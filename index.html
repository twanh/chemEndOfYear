    This chemistry year has definitely taught me a lot in term of chemistry and science in general. Not only have I learned about a variety of chemistry topics but also about scientific thinking and reasoning. This is not only useful during chemistry but also during physics.   • whatshot In society (imo): Click me!

    The role of chemistry but more science in general in society in my opinion is quite big. Everything we know is based on chemistry, physics and/or biology. The role of society in me learning chemistry is not that big, of course because chemistry is well supported in the netherlands and accepted as a important science there is a lot of support and being good/knowing chemistry in this current society might bring a lot of opportunities. But since I am not that interested in pursuing a career in chemistry that does not play a role in my personal development in chemistry.

    Having said that I do believe chemistry is an important subject. Especially since it does help you understand a lot of important concepts to understand to world we live in. Which definitely something I am interested in. This year my understanding of chemistry definitely has grown and so had my interest in it. Before, I thought of chemistry as just another subject but I have grown to actually quite like it and find the topics discussed interesting (not to say I am good at it).
